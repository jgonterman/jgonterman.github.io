My Resume

My name is Jeremy Gonterman, and I have spent 18 years involved in music. Throughout that time, I have learned 5 instruments (trumpet, guitar, bass, piano, and drums), played in ensembles that have traveled the country and internationally, and owned my own mixing / mastering business. I graduated from Missouri Western State University in 2019 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Audio Engineering and have taken 9 years’ worth of music theory courses. In 2019 I sold my company and joined Pepsi as a Bulk Customer Representative. These days I am an Independent Account Manager and MBA student at the University of Missouri. I am also seeking a Marketing certificate, with an estimated graduation date of December 2024.
