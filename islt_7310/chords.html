A chord is made up of at least 3 tones. They are built up from the bottom note, the root. The most basic chords are called triads. The intervals between a Major Triad are Root (1), 3, 5.
- For example, if we use the Eb Major Scale from the Scales lesson, an Eb Major Chord would be Eb, G, Bb.
